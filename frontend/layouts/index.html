About Me

Howdy, welcome to my corner of the internet. Where I document the things I’ve built, broken, and occasionally fixed while incorrectly assuming I knew what was going on the whole time. This blog isn’t so much a carefully curated collection of wisdom as it is a curious, haphazardly assembled set of projects, conquests, and fumbles made through my journey as student, engineer, and an editor of the hitch hikers guide to the galaxy.

So, if you’re looking for answers to life’s biggest questions, I can’t promise those. But if you’re here for something a little more chaotic, you’ve definitely found the right spot. And if you find something perplexing along the way? Don’t panic. That’s just part of the ride.
